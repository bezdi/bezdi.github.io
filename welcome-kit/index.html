Transportation

To and from the Park
By trains and buses we organize
  • Trains go every hour from Budapest Nyugati to Albertirsa (9 km from the Park)
  • Suggested train times: 9:00, 10:00, or 11:00 AM (Sat & Sun)
  • County24h for Pest county ticket: 999 HUF; EU/EEA/Swiss citizens aged 65+ travel free (excluding airport bus 100E)
  • Arrive 25 minutes early at Nyugati (e.g. 9:35)
  • Rented buses available from 10:00–12:00 AM from Albertirsa to the Park
  • After train arrival, turn right in the underpass to minibuses (signposted)
  • Return buses from Park: 20:30–22:15 both evenings
  • Last train: 23:40 to Budapest; 23:44 to Cegléd
  • Notify organizers if leaving early or late
If you miss the bus
  • Public yellow bus from Albertirsa to Mikebuda at 12:10 – say “Bernula tanya” to the driver
  • If you cannot find a bus, call Balázs: +36 30 651 6317

More trains run on weekdays, but not all have connections to the Park

How to survive in Hungary

Weather

In the Park it’s usually colder and windier than in Budapest. The nights can be chilly, so please bring some warm clothes with you.

Currency

The local currency is Hungarian Forints (HUF). In Budapest, most places accept card. Large shops sometimes accept Euros, but exchange rates are poor. You can pay for registration and meals with Euros in the Park if necessary.

  • Many ATMs available in central Budapest and some in Albertirsa.
  • Best exchange rates around Nyugati Railway Station.
  • Few exchange offices in Cegléd, none in Albertirsa.
  • Avoid exchanging money at the airport — use cards instead.
Safety
  • Beware of pickpockets in Budapest.
  • Only use taxis from official companies.
  • Watch out for cars and bikes — pedestrians have low priority.
Prices in general
  • Soft drinks: 700-800 HUF (1.5–2 EUR)
  • Cigarettes: 2200–2300 HUF (5.5–6 EUR)
  • Taxi rides: ~5000 HUF (12 EUR) in Budapest
  • Meal at restaurant: ~5000 HUF (13 EUR)
Cigarettes/Tobacco

Only sold in official "Nemzeti Dohánybolt" (National Tobacco Shop).

Nearest supermarket and tobacco shop to the Park is in Albertirsa (9 km).
